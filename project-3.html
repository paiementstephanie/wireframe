Synesthesia Stop Motion Animation

This is tactile project was made to illustrate a snippet of how I see the world through my Synesthesia. Senses overlap and create hybrids, where there are many different types. Mine are that I see sounds, put personalities and colours to numbers and letters, and sometimes I taste visions. For this Project I focused solely on the music portion of my abilities.
