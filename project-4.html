The Bathers

This piece was one of my works in my 2012 Praxis Grad Show for my Bachelor of Fine Arts at the University of Ottawa. The Bathers won the Paradigme Gallery prize and was purchased by the University. This painting was apart of my series entitled Repetitive Reductions which received the Best in Show award.

Acrylic on paper,40x50 inches, 2012.
