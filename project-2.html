Eco Friendly Nintendo DS Packaging

For this packaging assignment, the objective was to make an environmentally friendly version of something. I chose a Nintendo 3DS game package that is made by recyclable paper materials. The final project used 90% less plastic. Where there's only one small plastic game holder inside disguised as an item block!
